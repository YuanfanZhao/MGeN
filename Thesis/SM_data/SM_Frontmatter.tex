This supplementary material contains four special cases of Ge-N$_d$ distribution family, and introduces their definitions, basic properties, statistical inference together with the corresponding mean regression models in detail. At the same time, we added some US algorithms we constructed and the related distributions we need to use. Some numerical experimental results not presented in the article will also be supplemented in this document.
